What's CropConnect ?

CropConnect is a non-profit platform focused on providing meaningful, short-term, volunteer farming experiences in your local area. If you are a farmer in need of help, you can visit our website and post what you need help with. If you are looking for an active and impactful experience, you can enter where your location, and we show you all the farmers from the closest to the furthest. Our goal is to encourage community support and bring neighbourhoods across the globe closer together.

How to contact CropConnect ?

You can reach CropConnect at hello@cropconnect.world.
